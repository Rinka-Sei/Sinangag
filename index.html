Garlic Fried Rice (Sinangag)

A quintessential Filipino breakfast staple, Sinangag is fragrant, garlicky rice that pairs perfectly with eggs, meat, or enjoyed on its own. It's also a fantastic way to use leftover rice!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cooked white rice (day-old is best!)
  • 4-6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2-3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: Green onions, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat the cooking oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic and saut√© until golden brown and fragrant. Be careful not to burn it!
  3. Add the cooked rice to the skillet. Break up any clumps with a spatula.
  4. Stir constantly to distribute the garlic and oil evenly throughout the rice.
  5. Season with salt to taste. Continue stirring and cooking for 5-7 minutes, or until the rice is heated through and slightly toasted.
  6. If using, garnish with chopped green onions before serving. Serve hot and enjoy!
